Extra Crispy Parmesan-Crusted Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • Baby Potatoes
  • Neutral Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Parprika
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Parrmesan

    • Instructions

    1. Cut the baby potatoes in half
    2. Add them into a medium sized (preferably metal) bowl
    3. Add a generous amount of oil into the bowl with the potatoes. Toss them and make sure they are all coated evenly
    4. Add in all the seasoning. Add enough so that each potatoes has seasoning
    5. Cover the bowl with clibg wrap, and let it marinate in the fridge for a minimum of 30 minutes
    6. As potatoes are marinating, preheat oven to 400 F
    7. Cover a tray that will fit in your oven and fit all the potatoes with a thin layer of parmesan
    8. Once potatoes are marinated, place them flat side down onto the paremsan
    9. Once oven is preheated, place tray in oven and bake for 30 minutes. Make sure to watch them as they cook
    10. Once they have fully cooked and gotten crispy, take them out and let them cool for 5 minutes before serving
    11. Enjoy!
