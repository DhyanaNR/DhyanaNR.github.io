Garlic Chilly Fried Rice

Ingredients:

  • Neutral Oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Cooked Rice
  • Chopped Carrots
  • Chopped Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasame Oil
  • Garlic Chilli Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Green Onions *optional*
  • Garlic Chips *optional*

    • Instructions

    1. On your stove set to low heat, add oil, garlic and ginger to your wok and saute until lightly browned
    2. Add chopped carrots and onions to the wok and continue to stir
    3. Add in cooked and cooled white jasmine rice. Turn the heat up the stove to medium heat, and combine the veggies and rice in the wok
    4. Add in Soy Sauce, Seasame Oil, and Chilli Oil. Estimate how much of the sauses to use based on the how brwn you like your fried rice
    5. Stir the fried rice and season with salt and pepper. Cook on medium heat for 2-3 minutes
    6. Serve hot and top with green onions and garlic chips
    7. Enjoy!
