Pasta Ala Vodka

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Neutral Oil
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Tomatoe Paste
  • Heavy Creame
  • Oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chilli Flakes
  • Parmesan
  • Bread Crumbs *optional*

    • Instructions

    1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Add in pasta and stir occasionally
    2. While pasta is cooking, begin on the sauce:
    3. In a large pan on low heat, add oil and cook chopped garlic
    4. Add in tomatoe paste and cook until paste appears to be darker in color
    5. Slowly pour in heavy creame and stir until tomatoe past is combined
    6. Season with oregano, salt, pepper, and chilli flakes
    7. Add in parmesan and mix
    8. Once pasta is cooked to al dente, add the pasta to the pan. Make sure that it is on low-medium heat
    9. Slowly mix the pasta, and gradually increase the heat
    10. Serve and heat hot. Top with bread crumbs and extra parmesan if wanted
    11. Enjoy!
